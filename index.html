AIMS7 ERP for SP

Featured Services

AIMS7 SP app does everything you could possibly think! Here are most user-friendly, beautiful, information-based and popular solution for you.

Mobile Apps

We offer adaptive mobile applications designed by AIMS7 experts including offline billing features

MikroTik Integrated

The most valuable features for ISP. AIMS7 SP is configured and can exchange information through MikroTik API

Billing Management

Get auto-generated invoices daily, weekly, monthly, Yearly, and/ or as per joining date and manage all your collections.

Accounts Management

Manage all your daily, monthly, yearly income & expense with details reporting including transaction through apps.

Resellers Management

Manage your prepaid/postpaid resellers commission, customers, billing, etc. Reseller can manage his customers with a separate login through our software and apps.

Multiple Branch & Staff Management

Having multiple branch management facilities with Multiple staff recruitment under each branch with their role management for software and apps.

Area Management

Having an area management facility. The assigned staff of every area can manage his customer's service & activities.

Multi Login

Having login facility for admin, staff, customers, and resellers with apps and software to manage his activities.

Service Management

Our cutting-edge service management tool helps you and your customers to manage the service request without any delays both from software and apps.

Unmetered Cloud Storage

AIMS7 SP maintain a cloud database to manage your data. You can access anytime and anywhere by software or apps, even if you are in offline.

Inventory Management

Manage all your accessories and other products easily. You can manage your sales, purchase, vendors, customers and Accounts using our software.

Payroll

Configure salary structure, Holidays, designation, giving salary to your employees, Attendance & manage their account is easily manageable by our software

Mobile / Bluetooth Printer

Having auto printed receipts facility remotely for every customers payment using Bluetooth / mobile printer with our apps, even if you are offline.

ID card / Invoice Printing

Having QR code printed auto generated ID card & Invoice for every month. So, you can print ID and invoice for every customer at any time.
